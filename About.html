Hi, I'm Fulvia

I'm a web designer and developer based in Fort Collins, Colorado. I build custom websites from scratch with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — the kind where every detail is considered, from the first sketch to the final line of code.

What I love most is creating digital spaces where color, animation, and content come together to make people feel something. I'm the rare developer who actually enjoys tackling complicated technical problems and has an eye for design.

I also work with WordPress and Wix Studio when they're the right fit. Whatever the platform, I build sites for creative businesses and small teams who care about how their work is presented to the world.

Creative Vision

I don't just build websites — I design experiences. Every project starts with understanding what you want people to feel.

Technical Depth

Hand-coded HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Clean, performant code. SEO optimization. I handle the complex stuff so you don't have to.

Clear Communication

I translate technical jargon into plain language. You'll always know what's happening and why.

Beyond the Screen

Before I found web design, I spent years in academia as a philosopher — which taught me how to think systematically about complex problems. That background shapes how I approach every project: methodically, thoughtfully, and always asking "why" before "how."

When I'm not at my computer, I'm a ceramic artist working with raku firing techniques, and a photographer. The same attention to craft and detail that goes into my art goes into every website I build.

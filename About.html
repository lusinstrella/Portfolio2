About Me

I am a web designer and multimedia artist based in Fort Collins, Colorado, with a unique background blending academic research, design thinking, and artistic practice.

After years in academia as a philosopher, I transitioned to web design and UX/UI, earning a certificate in Fundamentals of Multimedia Technology and completing a UX/UI Bootcamp at the University of Denver. My expertise lies at the intersection of modern CSS, responsive design, and user-centered problem-solving.

I bring over 20 years of professional experience in research, critical thinking, and creative expression to every project. My work combines technical precision with artistic vision, creating digital experiences that are both functional and visually compelling.

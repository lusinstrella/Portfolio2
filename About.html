Hi, my name is Fulvia and I am a web designer and multimedia artist based in Fort Collins, Colorado.
I am also a philosopher and spent many years teaching, researching and writing in academia.

The COVID-19 pandemic and the birth of a new child changed my life, giving me the opportunity to reconsider my choices.
I decided to go back to school and fulfill my lifelong dream of becoming an artist.
I enrolled in the local community college where - in addition to taking all the studio art classes I could possibly fit in my schedule - I earned a certificate in Fundamentals of Multimedia Technology.

During that time I had the fortune of taking a Web Design course with Geoff Graham, front-end developer and senior editor of Smashing magazine.
Thatâ€™s when I fell in love with CSS.

CSS stands at that irresistible intersection between graphic design, technological wonder, and language learning.
The decision to enroll in a UX/UI Bootcamp at the University of Denver, seemed like a logical sequitur.
The course gave me the opportunity to experiment with new tools and deepened my understanding of both design theory and coding.

When I am not building websites, you can find me making and teaching ceramics at the Fort Collins Pottery studio, biking around town, often with my husband and children, knitting or playing with my three cats.

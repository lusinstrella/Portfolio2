Inspiration

For this project I wanted to highlight on the connection between the coffee we drink and the plant that grows in nature.

I tried to evoke the experience of smelling the aroma of coffee and being transported into a place of comfort and pleasure. I kept thinking of a coffee shop in Vienna, where I sat once, waiting for a train. They roasted their coffee in the back and the front was elegant and slightly melancholic.

The Problem

This was the original website. I needed to veer off commonly seen coffee branding, populated with burly Brooklyn hipsters, roasting coffee in hyper modern rooms.

The Logo

My first problem was the logo: it didn't fit with the overall vibe I had in mind. I created a new one, evoking the idea of coffee as tradition and ritual.
It's a logo that makes me think of home or of an old neighborhood coffee shop where people go have breakfast every morning.

Creating a Pattern

Next I designed a delicate pattern of coffee leaves and beans, to use as a background image.
My intention was to create the subtle impression of a turn of the century wallpaper.

A Small Touch of Delight

Finally, I created a whimsical animation for the footer. The animation is subtle and triggered on hover. This prevents the users from feeling overwhelmed and gives them complete control.

Responsiveness

I coded my website, using some Bootstrap elements and utility classes. In other cases it made more sense to make my own grids and then add a few snippets of media queries. The final result is a dynamic website that is responsive for large screens, desktops, tablets and mobile phones.

GitHub

I published the website on GitHub, which allows me to keep track of all the versions I have made so far. It also makes it very easy to push any future changes

Keeping up with CSS

CSS is a constantly evolving language. New features are being introduced all the time. I like staying on top of it and trying new things. That's way the colors on the websites are defined in the Oklch environment, while I use the dynamic viewport for all the main sizing on the site.

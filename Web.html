Inspiration

For this project I wanted to highlight on the connection between the coffee we drink and the plant that grows in nature.

I tried to evoke the experience of smelling the aroma of coffee and being transported into a place of comfort and pleasure.

I also wanted to veer off burly Brooklyn hipsters, roasting coffee in hyper modern rooms.

I kept thinking of a coffee shop in Vienna, where I sat once, waiting for a train.
They roasted their coffee in the back and the front was elegant and slightly melancholic.

The Logo

I created a logo that evokes the idea of coffee as tradition and ritual. It's a logo that makes me think of home or of an old neighborhood coffeeshop where people go have breakfast every morning.

Karl Dukstein Photography

A bold, minimalist portfolio showcasing nature and street photography — where every image tells a story.

Role: Designer & Developer Year: 2024 Tech: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Visit Live Site ↗ View Code

Overview

Karl Dukstein is a photographer specializing in capturing raw moments in nature and urban environments. His work needed a website that would let the photography speak for itself without distraction.

I designed a minimalist portfolio that puts the focus entirely on the images, with a bold, clean layout that creates breathing room around each photograph. The result is a site that feels like walking through a gallery rather than browsing a website.

Image-First Design

Created a layout that prioritizes visual impact, with large, full-bleed images and minimal text interference to let the photography command attention.

Minimal Navigation

Designed an unobtrusive navigation system that stays out of the way until needed, maintaining focus on the work while remaining accessible.

Performance Optimization

Implemented lazy loading and optimized image delivery to ensure fast load times without compromising on visual quality.

Overview

Karl Dukstein is a documentary photographer whose work captures raw, powerful moments — from the oil fields of Frack County to political events like the DNC. He needed a portfolio that would let his images speak while providing easy navigation across multiple series.

I built a minimal, editorial-style site that puts the photography front and center. The dark interface recedes into the background, letting viewers focus entirely on the work. A custom dropdown navigation makes it easy to browse between series without cluttering the page.

Key Highlights

Image-First Design

Full-screen slideshow presentation with minimal UI. The photography takes center stage with no distractions.

Series Navigation

Custom dropdown menu organizing multiple photo series — Frack County, DNC, Portraits, and more — in a clean, intuitive structure.

Editorial Feel

Dark, understated design that matches the serious tone of documentary work. Typography and spacing inspired by print publications.

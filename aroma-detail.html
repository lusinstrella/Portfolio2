Overview

For this project I wanted to highlight the connection between the coffee we drink and the plant that grows in nature. I tried to evoke the experience of smelling the aroma of coffee and being transported into a place of comfort and pleasure.

I kept thinking of a coffee shop in Vienna, where I sat once, waiting for a train. They roasted their coffee in the back and the front was elegant and slightly melancholic. That feeling guided every design decision.

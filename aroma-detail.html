Aroma Coffee Roasters

A warm, inviting website for a local coffee roaster — designed to evoke the aroma and comfort of their craft.

Role: Designer & Developer Year: 2024 Tech: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Visit Live Site ↗ View Code

Overview

For this project I wanted to highlight the connection between the coffee we drink and the plant that grows in nature. I tried to evoke the experience of smelling the aroma of coffee and being transported into a place of comfort and pleasure.

I kept thinking of a coffee shop in Vienna, where I sat once, waiting for a train. They roasted their coffee in the back and the front was elegant and slightly melancholic. I needed to veer off commonly seen coffee branding, populated with burly Brooklyn hipsters, roasting coffee in hyper modern rooms.

Visual Identity

Created a new logo evoking the idea of coffee as tradition and ritual — a logo that makes me think of home or of an old neighborhood coffee shop where people go have breakfast every morning.

Custom Pattern Design

Designed a delicate pattern of coffee leaves and beans to use as a background image, creating the subtle impression of turn of the century wallpaper.

Thoughtful Animation

Created a whimsical animation for the footer that is subtle and triggered on hover, preventing users from feeling overwhelmed and giving them complete control.
